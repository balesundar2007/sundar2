RAJGAD FORT

Rajgad Fort, the "King of Forts," was the first capital of the Maratha Empire, strategically located on the Sahyadri range near Pune, known for its immense size, complex fortifications (Padmavati Machi, Sanjivani Machi, Balekilla), water systems, and ruins of palaces and offices, making it a significant historical site and popular trekking destination with breathtaking views. It was originally called Murumdeo and offers a challenging but rewarding trek with historical structures and natural beauty.
